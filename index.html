About Me

I’m a multi-disciplinary UX designer who blends product thinking, Visual Design motion to create memorable digital experiences.

My Story

I started as a visual designer, obsessed with layout, type and color. Over time I moved into UX, learning how research, data and interaction patterns shape real products. To push my work further, I learned 3D and motion so I can prototype interfaces in more expressive ways.

Today I help teams go from idea to launch: mapping user flows, designing high-fidelity UI, building functional prototypes, and producing motion and 3D assets that match the brand.
